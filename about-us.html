About us

Welcome to Luxury Loom Interiors

At Luxury Loom Interiors, we believe in creating spaces that inspire, function and reflect your unique style. With years of experience in the interior design industry, our passionate team combines creativity with a deep understanding of your needs.

Whether your space is residential or a commercial transformation we work closely with you every step of the way ensuring every detail is thoughtfully considered.

Our mission is to deliver luxurious interiors that reflect the unique vision and lifetsyle of our clients that is not only beautiful but also meaningful and practical.

Luxury Loom Interiors was founded by Anatasia Blackwood and co-founder Elizabeth Sinclair in 2014. Expanding with 15 talented interior designers who bring wealth of expertise to the business. Since then it has become the UK's leading interior design business.
